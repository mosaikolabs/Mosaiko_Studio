Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working together and the project process.

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical website redesign takes 6-8 weeks, while brand identity projects usually require 4-6 weeks. I provide detailed timelines during the proposal phase, including key milestones and review periods.

I follow a structured 5-phase process: Discovery & Strategy, Concept Development, Design & Iteration, Development & Testing, and Launch & Optimization. Each phase includes client collaboration, regular check-ins, and approval gates to ensure we're aligned throughout the project.

All projects include strategy consultation, design concepts, revisions, final files, and 30 days of post-launch support. Development projects also include responsive coding, basic SEO optimization, and content management system setup. Detailed pricing is provided in the project proposal.
